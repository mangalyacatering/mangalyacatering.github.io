About Us

Mangalya is one of India’s leading event management company based in Bangalore. We are celebrating over 35 years in event business. Since 1984 we have been serving the finest food and creating the most engaging atmosphere for our guests. Our proven track record with extreme client satisfaction gives us an upper hand in the industry. We offer 360 degrees from creative ideas to complete production and event management services.

We offer complete solutions for Catering and Event Management, offering interactive and personalized culinary experiences. We have a well equipped kitchen and an amazing back end to pull together our events along with the reputation, creativity, diverse skill set and attention to detail to ensure a grand success of your event. Experience The Art Of Fine Catering and the comfort of Being A Guest At Your Own Event all under one roof.

We make it happen, the way you want it to happen!
